Course Completion Day

Main Ceremony

8:00 AM 9:25 AM
Saturday 31 May, 2025

The program starts with participant registration, followed by an introduction and the Cambodian National Anthem led by the MC. The class monitor presents the course progress, and the teacher educator shares impressions and reports the results. A trainee delivers an impression speech about the course, followed by remarks from the center director. The teacher educator then hands out academic records, and the event concludes with a photo session.

Party

9:25 AM 12:00 PM
Saturday 31 May, 2025

We organized a fun party where we cooked food ourselves and enjoyed a variety of delicious dishes. There was plenty of juice to drink, and everyone had a great time dancing and singing together. It was a joyful and lively event filled with laughter, music, and good memories.
